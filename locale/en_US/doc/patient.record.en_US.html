The core EMR of FreeMED is located in the "patient" table of the database, and is split into several logical sections, which are represented as "tabs" when adding or modifying a patient record.
If you do not wish to see the multiple tabs while registering a patient and prefer to have one large entry screen, click on Main -> Administrative Menu -> System Configuration -> Fold Multipage Forms?, choose "no" and then click on "Configure" on the bottom of the screen.

Registration Sections:

  • Primary Information - Name, address and date of birth.
  • Contact - Phone numbers, email address, fax number.
  • Personal - Gender, marital status, employment status, patient status, social security number, internal practice ID, drivers' license, billing type and budget.
  • Physician - In-house, referring, primary care, and other physicians.
  • Medical - Blood type, preferred pharmacy
  • Notes - General notes field.
