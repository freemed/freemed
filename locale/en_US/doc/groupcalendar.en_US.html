Group Calendar
This the calendar of the group that was chosen on the login screen. This daily calendar displays the calendar of all of the members in the group.
This calendar is useful for staff members to determine office flow patterns and to determine when office time is available for a particular provider.
  • To change the time of an appointment on the same day that is displayed or to change the provider who the patient will see that day, put the mouse on the blank space to right of the word "Move" and then drag the appointment to the desired location.
  • To change the date and time of the appointment to another day, click on the word "Move". This will take you to another screen where you can chose the desired date of the appointment. Then click on the desired time of the appointment. This will take you to another screen where you can change the note attached to the appointment and then you can click on "Confirm Booking"to set the appointment.
  • You can also book an appointment from this calendar. If you click on an open slot, you will be taken to a screen that allows you to choose the name of the patient and an associated appointment template. You have to choose a previously created template or the appointment won't be created properly. The creation of templates is discussed in the section "Appointment Templates" found by going to Main -> Support Data -> Appointment Templates.
  • Lastly, you can also choose to book non-patient related activities such as travel (the default), staff meetings, etc. First, click on the desired time on the calendar, choose the activity and then click on the length of time to create this type of appointment.
    The creation of templates for non-patient related activities is discussed by going to Main -> Administration Menu -> System Configuration -> Group Calendar.
