This is a feature that allows you to display the names of anesthesia providers at Ambulatory Care Centers.
At times, surgeons have preferences for certain anesthesia providers.
To display the name of the covering anesthesia provider, click on the date on the calendar when a specific anesthesia provider will be at the amulatory care center.
Choose the name of the site where the anesthesia provider will be working in the blank next to the words "Anesthesiology Scheduler for".
Next to the word "Book" choose the name of the provider.
Then click on the "Book" button.
The name of the covering anesthesia provider will now be shown on the top of the ambulatory care center calendar.
